About me

Designing better digital experiences through craft, clarity and care.

I’m a web and digital designer with over seven years of experience working in the B2B space. I take a multidisciplinary approach to design, blending branding, UX, visual design, and front-end development to create thoughtful, effective digital products.

My focus is on improving how users experience brands and platforms through research, close collaboration, and keeping an eye on real-world performance and trends. I’ve worked on award-winning digital products, including The World's 50 Best website that helped our team win “Product Development Team of the Year” at the 2024 Digital Publishing Awards.

I believe in working with purpose, growing through every project, and raising the bar in every collaboration. I care about good ideas, clear communication, and well-crafted work.

Outside of design, I’m into craft, nature and hanging out with my cat, Solly!
